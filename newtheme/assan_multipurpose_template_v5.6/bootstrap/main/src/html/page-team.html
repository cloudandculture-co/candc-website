About Us

Cloud and Culture was founded with a clear vision: to make cultural experiences accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world.

Our flagship product, Pocket Museum, brings together technology and culture to create immersive digital experiences that connect people with art, history, and heritage. Based in London, our team combines expertise in technology, museum curation, and storytelling to create engaging cultural content for the digital age.

Our Mission

At Cloud and Culture, we believe that cultural experiences should be accessible to everyone, regardless of geographical location, physical ability, or economic means. We're dedicated to breaking down barriers and creating digital pathways to art, history, and cultural heritage.

Through thoughtful technology and expert curation, we aim to enrich lives, foster understanding, and preserve cultural heritage for future generations.
